POWER(Math):
The power (or exponent) of a number says how many times to use the number in a multiplication.

Examples:

82 = 8 x 8 = 64 . In this example the little "2" says to use 8 two times in a multiplication.

Another example: 24 = 2 x 2 x 2 x 2 = 16 We can say "2 to the power 4 is 16", or we can also say "the 4th power of 2 is 16"
